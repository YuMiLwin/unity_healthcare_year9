Parkinson's Disease


Parkinson's disease is a neurological condition that affects movement. It can cause symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and difficulty with balance and coordination.

Health Impact


It can lead to movement difficulties and may affect speech and mental functions.

Prevention and Management


Medications, physical therapy, and lifestyle changes can help manage symptoms.
