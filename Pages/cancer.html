Cancer


Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells in the body. These cells can form tumors and interfere with normal bodily functions. Cancer can occur in various organs and tissues, and there are over 100 different types. The exact cause of cancer is often complex, involving genetic, environmental, and lifestyle factors.

What are the symptoms of cancer?
