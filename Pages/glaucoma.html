Galucoma


Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often due to increased intraocular pressure. It is a leading cause of irreversible blindness and typically has no early symptoms. As it progresses, glaucoma can result in tunnel vision and, eventually, complete vision loss.

What are the symptoms of Galucoma?


  • vision loss
  • blindness

How can we prevent?


    Regular eye exams and early detection can help prevent vision loss. Treatment may include medication or surgery to lower intraocular pressure.
